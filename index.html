We are part of a global movement using advocacy, technology and training to unlock information, create and share open knowledge.

We enable open data creation

We support the creation of open data for institutions, organisations and communities that want to make the best decisions based on evidence.

We simplify reproducible research

We work to ensure that knowledge reaches all communities in a collaborative, open, and standardised way, making crucial data used and useful for the public interest.

We are building a global movement

We equip people with data tools and capabilities and connect them with open knowledge communities worldwide.
